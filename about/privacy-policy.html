privacy policy

Last updated: 25.10.2024

At Rainydays, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website and shop for our jackets. By using our site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information we collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with a better shopping experience:

Personal Information: When you make a purchase, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us through the website, we may collect information such as your name, email address, mailing address, and payment details.

Non-Personal Information: We may collect data on how you interact with our website, including your IP address, browser type, device type, and browsing behavior (e.g., which pages you visit and how long you spend on them).

How we use your information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Order Processing: To fulfill and ship your orders, manage payments, and provide customer service.

Marketing and Communications: To send you newsletters, promotional offers, and product updates if you've opted in.

Website Improvement: To analyze how visitors use our website and to improve our services, products, and user experience.

Legal Compliance: To comply with any legal obligations or protect our rights.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

Cookies: Small data files stored on your browser that help us remember your preferences and improve website functionality.

Analytics: We use tools like Google Analytics to track website traffic and analyze user behavior.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

Third-Party Services

We may share your personal data with third-party services to enhance our operations:

Payment Processors: We use secure payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to handle transactions.

Shipping Providers: To ensure your jackets are delivered promptly.

Marketing Platforms: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may use third-party platforms (e.g., Mailchimp) to send communications.

We ensure that these third parties comply with data protection laws and only use your information as needed to perform their services.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access the personal information we hold about you.

Rectify any inaccurate information.

Request Deletion of your data, except for information we are legally obligated to retain.

Withdraw Consent for marketing communications at any time by unsubscribing from our newsletter or contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

RainyDays does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us to remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Any modifications will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Contact Us
