Description

Designed for long-distance hikers and explorers, the Trek jacket is built to handle the rigors of the trail. Its durable construction and weatherproof technology ensure you stay comfortable and protected even in the harshest conditions. Featuring reinforced panels in high-wear areas and adjustable features to customize your fit, this jacket keeps you moving without restriction.

Stay cool with its ventilation system on tough uphill climbs, and seal out the wind and rain when the weather turns. The Trek jacket is all about endurance, just like your spirit of adventure.

  • Care: Machine washable, tumble dry on low
