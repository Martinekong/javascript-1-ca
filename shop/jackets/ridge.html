Description

The Ridge jacket is built for mountain adventurers who need maximum protection from the elements. Featuring a windproof shell and a fleece-lined interior, this jacket is made for colder conditions where warmth and durability are key. Its high-collar design and adjustable cuffs provide extra coverage, while the breathable fabric prevents overheating during high-output activities.

Whether you're skiing, snowboarding, or tackling a challenging climb, the Ridge jacket is your ultimate defense against cold, wind, and snow.

  • Pockets: Zippered hand and chest pockets for storage
  • Fit: Regular fit with adjustable cuffs and hem
  • Extras: Snow skirt and goggle wipe in pocket
  • Care: Machine washable, tumble dry low
