Description

When the wind picks up and the weather gets unpredictable, the Cyclone jacket steps in to protect. Built to shield you from high winds and light rain, it combines a lightweight build with durable windproof materials. The interior lining wicks away sweat, ensuring you're as dry on the inside as you are shielded from the elements on the outside.

Perfect for those fast-paced hikes, runs, or biking adventures in windy conditions, the Cyclone is as adaptable as your next adventure.

Product Details

  • Material: Windproof nylon with a breathable mesh lining
  • Fit: Slim fit with articulated elbows for flexibility
  • Pockets: Zippered chest pocket and side hand pockets
  • Ventilation: Mesh panels under the arms for enhanced airflow
  • Care: Machine washable, air dry for best results
